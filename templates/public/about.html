About TEVISA

Fostering athletic development and competition among technical and vocational institutions across Zambia.

Our Mission

Promote sports participation and physical education among technical and vocational students in Zambia, encouraging personal growth and fitness.

Our Vision

Enabling access to quality sports programs for every student, empowering them to compete in national and international competitions.

Objectives

  • Organize annual sports tournaments for institutions across Zambia.
  • Provide a platform for students to showcase their sports talents.
  • Promote competition, teamwork, and fair play.
  • Foster collaboration among institutions through sports.
  • Enhance student health through regular physical activities.
